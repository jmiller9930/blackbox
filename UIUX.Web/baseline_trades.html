Baseline trades report

One row per closed trade (trade_id) from ledger fills—there are no open-position rows here. Held (mid-trade, position still open) exists only on the dashboard trade chain, not in this table. The Duration column is how long the position was open until this close, not “held” state. Lifecycle trade_id (bl_lc_…) is derived from the entry bar; market_event_id on each row is the exit bar. Policy TRADE / NO TRADE classifies this close row (orthogonal to idle NO TRADE vs held on the chain). Active baseline position is above when present. Click a row for snapshot. QEL is cohort evaluation—see the QEL page.

PnL column: gross model USD: (exit − entry) × size in quote terms. Not “return on full bankroll”—Sean sizing builds notional from risk slice × leverage (see Risk $ vs Bankroll $). PnL shows % vs notional in parentheses. Not net of fees, funding, or slippage. Full wording: API meta.pnl_semantics after load.

Dates are UTC. Apply runs the query. Scope filters policy classification on the ledger close, not lifecycle held (open position—that is trade chain only).
