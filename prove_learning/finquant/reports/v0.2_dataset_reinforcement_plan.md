## Coverage themes (must appear across the patch)

1. **Funding direction** — long vs short payment; sign flips with premium/discount.
2. **Who pays whom** — longs pay shorts vs shorts pay longs depending on rate sign and convention.
3. **Positive funding & long cost** — positive funding can **increase** carry cost for longs (paying side semantics).
4. **Negative funding & short cost** — symmetric reasoning for shorts.
5. **Mark vs index** — mark price for PnL/liquidation vs index for funding component (venue-dependent).
6. **Basis** — perp vs spot premium; not “always bullish.”
7. **Open interest & squeeze** — OI spike ≠ deterministic liquidation cascade; causal humility.
8. **Liquidation distance** — function of leverage, maintenance margin, **mark** volatility.
9. **Leverage impact** — amplifies liquidation proximity; funding **not** negligible at high leverage when cumulative.
10. **Holding cost vs EV** — carry/funding as drag vs signal EV; RSI oversold does **not** remove liquidation risk.

## False-claim inventory (non-exhaustive)

Embedded as **[VERIFY]** scenarios in `input`, rejected in `output`:

- Long perps always earn funding / always positive funding for longs  
- Positive funding is always bullish for longs  
- Liquidation risk negligible if RSI oversold  
- Mark price equals last traded price  
- High OI always confirms trend  
- Funding negligible at high leverage  
- Profitable last candle ⇒ valid entry ignoring liquidation distance  
- (Plus rotated variants in generator seed)
